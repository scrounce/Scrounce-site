the artist behind the color

Based in Waco, Texas, H Lewis is a digital artist and musician merging industrial aesthetics with organic storytelling. For over five years, the "Scrounce" project has served as an umbrella for creative exploration—from the intricate details of digital illustration to the sonic landscapes of the Unseen Heroes EP.

The work often finds beauty in the resilient; a fusion of sharp, modern graphic design and the raw reality of the human experience. Whether through a limited apparel drop or a 9-track production, the goal remains the same: to create pieces that feel like a signature.

— H Lewis
